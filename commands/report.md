# /report - Report Out-of-Scope Work

**When NOT to use:** For status updates (use your .hive/bee-N.md file instead).

Add to `plans/INBOX.md` under `## Pending`:

```markdown
### From [Your Role] (YYYY-MM-DD)
**Found in:** [current plan or context]
**Issue:** [one sentence description]
**Scope:** [what should be done]
**Urgency:** Blocking | Soon | Debt
```

Then continue your current work. **Do not act on this issue.**
